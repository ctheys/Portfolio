The Royal Australian Navy

Redesign of The Royal Australian Navy website which informs and supports marines and their families.

The Problem

The Australian Royal Navy's website - https://www.navy.gov.au/ has usability, responsive and design issues and we have been tasked to determine and introduce changes.


The Solution

We needed to research the UI pain points the user had while interacting with the website. In order to fullfil its purposes (informative, supportive and recruiting platform) we believe the information architecture and the interface design needed some work.

Role

UX/UI Designer

Research, Heuristic Evaluation, Information Architecture, Wireframing, Prototyping & User Interface, Testing & Iterations

Project Timeline

3 weeks

Tools

Figma, Miro, Google Survey
